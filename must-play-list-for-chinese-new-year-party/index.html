COASSF#44 - Music Play List for Chinese New Year Party

// under coassf

制作人：桂子山人

For the Stanford GSB Sloan Fellow Chinese New Year Party on this Friday, Feb 08，2013. The songs are picked for:

  1. Their festive and party spirit;
  2. Their iconic （many of them legendary） status in the Chinese music industry;
  3. Their ability to represent certain distinctive genres of Chinese music;
  4. Their coverage of the key Chinese regions such as Hong Kong, Taiwan, Southeast Asia, and Mainland. The audience for this party consists of 80 Sloan Fellows with their partners/children that come from 30+ countries all over the world with age ranging from 30 - 45 （roughly）.

Does it represent my taste? Of course not, but it's a great list for a party like Chinese New Year.

去虾米试听 (Play It)
