COASSF#09 - Stanford MBA vs Stanford Sloan Fellow (MSx)

Sloan Fellow (aka "MSx" since 2013) is a very unique program. It's in a league of its own with no other benchmark in this business. It's not an MBA, neither an EMBA. It's a 12-month residence program that grants you a Master of Science (in management) degree. Its tuition fee is the same as the two-year MBA program, and its academic rigor is much, much higher than any other EMBA or executive training program.
