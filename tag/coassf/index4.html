COASSF#09 - Stanford MBA vs Stanford Sloan Fellow (MSx)

Posted on 2012 09 06 Thursday in Stanford • Tagged with coassf • Leave a comment

Sloan Fellow (aka "MSx" since 2013) is a very unique program. It's in a league of its own with no other benchmark in this business. It's not an MBA, neither an EMBA. It's a 12-month residence program that grants you a Master of Science (in management) degree. Its tuition fee is the same as the two-year MBA program, and its academic rigor is much, much higher than any other EMBA or executive training program. For experienced professionals around my age group, Stanford's Sloan Fellow Program is the best and ideal
