COASSF#55 - GCBC Talk - Stay Full Stay Smart

Permalink: 2013-05-28 00:19:00 in Stanford tags: coassf

GCBC Talk Series: Stay Full, Stay Smart, by Herbert Yang

Date:     May 29, Wednesday

Time:     9-10pm

Venue:   Schwab Ferguson Lounge

Crunching Tiger, Hidden Dragon - how was the Gangnam Style Flash Mob born into GSB on Oct 12, 2012?

The Wedding Banquet - how to manage a rock band?

Hulk - a career from CFO to movie producer

Sense and Sensibility - how to tell stories with your pictures?

Eat Drink Man Woman - what are the drivers of your life

Pushing Hands - How to manage career transition?

Ride with the Devil - What do you bring to the table, as a leader?

Brokeback Mountain - What are the different goals in GSB, in Stanford, in any community

Lust, Caution - What exactly does a CFO do?

Life of Pi - Reflections of a sea turtle

The Days of Being Wild - The good, the Bad, the Ugly of running startups in Beijing

It'll be a casual talk. Given the crazy academic schedule this week, I don't expect more than 10-15 people to show up. I'll pick up some topics that the audience are most interested in, during this prolonged late coffee break. I'll have Ang Lee to take care of the business part, and maybe save Wong Kar Wai for the non-business part, for later.

Evidently, I was too bored.
